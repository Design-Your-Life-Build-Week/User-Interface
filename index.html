Features

Design Your Life is a unique application that is designed to improve your happiness, health, and overall quality of life. Design Your Life takes in some simple data, like your daily activities, and your mood, then it allows you to rate those activities inside a reflection log. These reflection logs are unique to your profile and will assist you in seeing which activities bring you the greatest enjoyment. This application was built with ReactJS and NodeJS and is brought to you by the students of Lambda.
